About Us

History

Founded in 2020, Saffron was born from a passion for blending traditional recipes with contemporary culinary techniques. Our founders, inspired by their travels and love for diverse cuisines, envisioned a dining experience that celebrates both the richness of heritage and the excitement of modern flavors. Since opening our doors, Saffron has become a beloved destination for food enthusiasts seeking quality, creativity, and warmth in every dish.

Mission

At Saffron, our mission is to deliver an exceptional dining experience by combining fresh, locally-sourced ingredients with innovative cooking methods. We strive to create a welcoming environment where every guest feels valued and every meal is a memorable journey of taste and tradition. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the plate, aiming to foster a sense of community and connection through our passion for food and hospitality.

Team

Our team is the heart of Saffron. Led by Executive Chef Vidhate Manjusha, whose culinary expertise is matched by her dedication to quality, our kitchen staff brings creativity and precision to every dish. The front-of-house team, guided by Manager Amar Shinde, ensures a seamless dining experience with exceptional service and genuine hospitality. Together, we are united by a shared passion for food and a commitment to making every visit to Saffron special.
